Narendra Damodardas modi

14th prime minister of India

Here's a time line of Narendra Modi's life:

  • 1950 - Born and raised in Vadnagar, a small town in northeastern Gujarat
  • 1995 - Narendra Modi was appointed as the Secretary of the BJP's national unit.
  • 1998 - Narendra Modi was promoted as General Secretary and held the position till October 2001.
  • 1978 - Modi received a Bachelor of Arts degree in political science from the School of Open Learning (SOL) at the University of Delhi,graduating with a third class.
  • 1983 - received a Master of Arts degree in political science from Gujarat University, graduating with a first class as an external distance learning student.
  • At 18years - Married to Jashodaben Chimanlal Modi.
  • 2001 to 2014 - He was the chief minister of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014 and is the Member of Parliament from Varanasi.
  • July 2007 - Modi completed 2,063 consecutive days as chief minister of Gujarat, making him the longest-serving holder of that post, and the BJP won 122 of 182 state-assembly seats in that year's election.
  • September 2013 - Modi was named the BJP's candidate for prime minister ahead of the 2014 Lok Sabha election.
  • 26th May, 2014 - Become Prime Minister of India
  • 27 May 2014 - Prime Minister Narendra Modi takes charge of the office of the Prime Minister of India, at South Block, in New Delhi
  • 2 October 2014 - Modi launched the Swachh Bharat Mission ("Clean India") campaign.
  • 3 October 2018 - Awarded annually by the United Nations Environmental Programme for the environmental leadership.
  • 24 September 2019 - Awarded annually by the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation for the global leadership.
